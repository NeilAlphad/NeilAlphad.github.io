My Project

The D'Grill Bite Ordering System is an informative website that functions similarly to popular food delivery platforms like Food Panda. However, it does not provide delivery services within the university premises. The website allows users to place their food orders in advance. Once the orders are prepared, customers can conveniently pick up their meals at the school canteen situated in Campus A, where D'Grill Bite is conveniently located. The website provides detailed information about the ordering process, menu options, and the location of the canteen. It aims to provide users with a seamless and efficient way to order their favorite meals while maximizing convenience within the university campus.
